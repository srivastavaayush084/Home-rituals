<h1 style="font-size:2.5rem; font-weight:800;">Return Policy</h1>

> **Effective Date:** July 10, 2026

---

## Eligible Returns

Returns are accepted only for:

- Wrong Product
- Damaged Product
- Manufacturing Defect

---

## Return Window

Return requests must be submitted within **48 hours** of delivery.

---

## Return Conditions

Products must be:

- Unused
- In Original Packaging
- With All Labels Intact
- Accompanied by Purchase Proof

---

## Return Shipping

If the return is due to our error, we will bear the shipping cost.

---

## Return Approval

Returns without prior approval may not be accepted.
