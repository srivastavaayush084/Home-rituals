<h1 style="font-size:2.5rem; font-weight:800;">Refund & Cancellation Policy</h1>

> **Effective Date:** July 10, 2026

---

## Order Cancellation

Orders may be cancelled before shipment.

Once dispatched, cancellation requests cannot be accepted.

---

## Eligible Refunds

Refunds are available if:

- Wrong product delivered
- Damaged product received
- Product lost during transit
- Duplicate payment made

---

## Refund Request

Please contact customer support within **48 hours** of delivery with:

- Order ID
- Product Photos
- Description of the issue

---

## Refund Timeline

Approved refunds are processed within **5–7 business days**.

---

## Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds are not available for:

- Change of mind
- Improper product usage
- Damage after delivery
- Missing original packaging

---

## Refund Approval

Every refund request is reviewed before approval.
